Learning in Networks Reading Group

Introduction:

Learning in Networks is a student-initiated reading group, where we are focused on the application of machine learning algorithms in computer networks. The aim of the group is to leverage potential collaborations and produce more promising solutions.

Regulation:

To enhance the efficiency of our group, we meet regularly and cover only 1 paper per meeting. Here are some additional regulations for both presentors and participants.

For Presentors:
  • Prepare materials for warming up before presenting (e.g., a 2 to 3 page review of the paper).
  • Restric the presentation to at most 15 slides.
For Participants:
  • Must read the matirial before meeting.
  • Prepare the questions or the research idea to discuss.
