I am a PhD candidate in the Department of Computer Science at the University of Victoria, BC, Canada, advised by Dr. Jianping Pan. I obtained my MSc in Computer Science from the same university, and BEng in Communication from Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi’an, China. My research is focused on the analysis and design of communication networks. I am especially interested in fundamental problems in learning and control.

Interests

  • Analysis and Optimization in Computer Networks
  • Online Learning
  • Multi-armed Bandits
