Bio

  • University of Victoria

I am a master student in the Department of Computer Science at University of Victoria, BC, Canada, supervised by Prof. Jianping Pan. I obtained my BEng in Communication from Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi’an, China. My research is focused on the analysis and design of communication networks. I am especially interested in fundamental problems in learning and control.
